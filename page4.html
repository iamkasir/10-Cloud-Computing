Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy is our priority. This Policy applies to the activities of Cloud Computing, Inc., a California-based business (together with its subsidiaries, affiliates, and any other related entities, “Cloud Computing”, “we”, “us”, “our”). This Policy describes how we may use, access, store, process, and disclose information that we obtain when our customers and users access or use our services and applications (the “Services”).

Use and Collection of Information

We may collect and store certain Personal Information about our customers and users for purposes of providing our Service. We may obtain Personal Information from individuals and from organizations who have a relationship with the individuals (such as employers, school administrators and third-party partners). The Personal Information may include name, email address, address, phone number, gender, age, and other information that could reasonably be used to identify a person.

We collect and use Non-Personal Information (such as IP addresses, device type and operating system, browser type and language, web referral pages and applications used) primarily to monitor and improve the quality of our Services and your user experience.

- Generate and review reports and analytics;

- Update customers and users about changes to our Services and policies, as well as other relevant information; and

- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share actual Personal Information or other information with third parties, including our business partners and service providers, for a variety of reasons, including to provide products and services, or to help develop or maintain our Services. For example, we may provide Personal Information to our hosting service provider so that they can host the Services.

We may also share Personal Information as required or permitted by applicable law and/or as necessary in order to protect the safety and security of our Services, of our customers and users, or of other persons, including to respond to legal proceedings, court orders, and governmental requests.

We may share Non-Personal Information with third parties, including our business partners and service providers, for the purposes listed above. We may also share Non-Personal Information in an aggregated form.

Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect our Services, our customers' and users’ information, information in transit, and processed data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. All data stored on our servers is encrypted and we routinely update our security measures to age-appropriate industry standards.

Data Retention

We store and process Personal Information we receive from users of our Services for as long as needed to provide our Services and to comply with applicable laws. After the end of the period of necessary retention, we delete the Personal Information as stated in our internal records retention policy.

Changes to Our Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. If we make material changes to the Policy, we will notify you by posting the changes on our website and/or by sending you an email. Your continued use of our Services after the Policy changes are effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
